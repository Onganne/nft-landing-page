Roadmap Process

With a Souless Service

PHASE 0

The Beginning of the Tweeting (Present)

This will be focusing on the growth of social media accounts, specifically Twitter. Tweets, shares, and posts to most of these social media accounts will be divided into posts of promoting the product by going into detail of the product's background and utility, by reviewing the tools and techniques used to produce the product (which includes reviewing the product itself and having a behind the scenes), and by asking for opinions to improve the product or increase variety of it. This is all to create a community, but of course the community must have somewhere to go, we need to lead them, which is going to be towards a discord channel at first, then a website. Creating a website takes priority, to give the viewers a more in-debt description of our product and a countdown timer. A discord server will be made after the website, this is to give them regular exclusive updates of the progress of our product. Meaning, what will be posted here will be both social media stuff and bonus content, like a drawing time lapse, how you draw, special events, and even thank you messages.

PHASE 0.5

Let's Start Sailing in the OpenSea (In a few days)

The first product to be released will be the Souless Scrubs NFT Collection, focusing on the Scrubs' random collection and drawing. It will be done on OpenSea due to the no gas-fee payment by using Polygon. However, this will be focusing on my product only, as OpenSea is too wide of a competition and also only fits the Souless Sketches type of NFT design, which is focusing more on utility rather than design. The special discord areas will have content about comparing our ratings to our competition and rating them as is. Our special page in the website will have the discord link and special page, also including an email list for them to have special offers notified via email.

PHASE 1

The Bear Market Adoption (In a few weeks)

During the NFT Bear Market, the Scrubs will be massively marketed and advertised for “adoption”. During this phase, this will test to see if people are willing to buy these “scrubs” NFT for the chance to earn something better in the future through the future phases, and in the present through utility, which comes in forms of getting alpha information, more access to the roadmap, more info to the team, upgradability of the scrub NFT, and exclusive offers following the roadmap.

First, note that we are trying to transfer to Weyu instead of Opensea. Second, note that this will happen once the NFT economy will be out of the bear market. Focusing on mass Collaborations, the main goal is to gather more exposure in the product.
